Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

GOGO Fleet ("we," "us," or "our") operates a fleet management platform and renter portal at gogofleet.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Renters: Full name, phone number, email address, driver's license number, delivery platform, and profile photos or documents you upload
  • Fleet owners and employees: Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Payment information: Payment transactions are processed by Stripe. We do not store your full card number. We store payment amounts, dates, methods, and Stripe session identifiers
  • Contract information: Rental agreement terms, contract dates, and electronic signatures
  • Vehicle information: Vehicle details, photos, maintenance records, and GPS tracking data if Bouncie integration is enabled

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage rental contracts and track payments
  • Send SMS notifications about your rental, payments, and account (via Twilio)
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Store and display documents you upload, including license and insurance card photos
  • Provide fleet owners with oversight of their vehicles and renters
  • Track vehicle locations when GPS integration is enabled

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. We implement row-level security to ensure fleet owners and their employees can only access their own fleet's data. Renter data is isolated per fleet and not shared between fleet owners.

Documents and photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage. Electronic signatures are stored as image files linked to your rental contract.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase — Database and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Twilio — SMS notifications (Privacy Policy)
  • Bouncie — GPS vehicle tracking, if enabled (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Maps — Map display for vehicle tracking (Privacy Policy)

5. SMS Communications

By providing your phone number as a renter, you consent to receive SMS messages from GOGO Fleet regarding your rental agreement, payment reminders, and account information. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by contacting your fleet manager.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your rental account is active or as needed to provide services. Payment records and signed contracts may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of SMS communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

GOGO Fleet is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
