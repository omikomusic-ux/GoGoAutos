Everything you need

Built specifically for gig-economy fleet owners

Not built for corporate fleets. Built for operators renting to DoorDash, UberEats, and delivery drivers.

Digital Contracts
Generate rental agreements instantly. Renters sign with their finger from their phone. Stored securely, accessible anytime.
Card Payments
Renters pay weekly rent directly from their portal by card. Payments auto-record. No more chasing cash or Venmo screenshots.
Automated SMS Reminders
Payment reminders fire automatically — day before, day of, and every day overdue. Late fees calculated instantly.
Renter Portal
Renters get their own portal at gogofleet.app/renter — see their contract, payment history, balance due, and pay online. Available in English, Spanish, and Vietnamese.
GPS Tracking
Live map showing every vehicle. Powered by Bouncie. Know where your fleet is at all times from your phone.
Fleet Calendar
Visual month view showing contract dates, expirations, and maintenance. See gaps in your schedule before they cost you revenue.
P&L Reports
Revenue, expenses, and net profit per vehicle. Weekly income reports. Fleet utilization. Everything your accountant needs.
Team Management
Add managers and viewers to your fleet. Each person sees only your data. Multiple fleet owners are fully isolated from each other.
Simple setup

Up and running in minutes

No technical knowledge required. If you can use your phone, you can run GOGO Fleet.

1
Create your account
Sign up with your email. Add your first vehicle — year, make, model, plate. Takes 2 minutes.
2
Add a renter
Enter their info, set contract terms, and tap Start Contract. A welcome text goes to their phone automatically.
3

They open gogofleet.app/renter, read the contract, and sign with their finger. No printing, no chasing.
4
Payments run themselves
Renters pay by card from their portal. Reminders fire automatically. Your dashboard updates in real time.
